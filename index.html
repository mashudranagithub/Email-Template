HEALTH SYSTEMS MANAGEMENT (JUNE 21)

The Master of Public Health (MPH) students at BRAC JPG School of Public Health have been engaged in active discussion, meaningful exchange of ideas and productive dialogue with health systems experts during the current Health Systems Management Module.

Student Achievements: Students have had collaborative effort in this course with successful presentations on various topics. Some of their experiential work include presentations on Protecting the Bangladeshi doctors, Community Health Workers in COVID-19 response Sierra Leone, How has covid-19 affected the UK doctors.

International Visiting Faculty:

  • Dr. Timothy Grant Evans, Inaugural Director and Associate Dean of the School of Population and Global Health (SPGH) in the Faculty of Medicine and Associate Vice-Principal at Global Policy and Innovation, McGill University
  • Ermin Erasmus, Collaboration for Health Policy & Systems Analysis in South Africa
  • Miatta Zenabu Gbanya, Immunization Specialist, UNICEF Afghanistan

HEALTH ECONOMICS AND HEALTH CARE FINANCING (JUNE 7)

The 5th online course during COVID-19 pandemic was the Health Economics and Health care financing.

Student Achievements: MPH students design community based innovative health insurance scheme

International Visiting Faculty: Dr. Jahangir Khan, Senior Lecturer at Department of International Public Health at Liverpool School of Tropical Medicine.
